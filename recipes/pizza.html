Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round,flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese,
and often various other ingredients(such as various types of sausage, anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, ham, etc.),
which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 400g flour
  • Ketchup
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Cheese
  • Salami or Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Basil
  • etc

Steps

  1. Mix the flour,yeast,sugar, and a bit of lukewarm water in a bowl.
  2. Knead the dough. Let it proof in a warm place for about an hour.
  3. Roll the dough with a rolling pin in the shape of your baking pan.
  4. Spread about a tbsp. of ketcup on the rolled out dough
  5. Add the cheese, pepperoni,mushrooms, and any other toppiings you'd like.
  6. Put the pizza in a pre-heated oven at 250 Â°C for about an hour.
  7. The pizza is done when the cheese melts and the dough has a light brownish color. Cut into triangular pieces. The pizza is done.
